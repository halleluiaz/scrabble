Welcome to Mini Scrabble!

Rules

  1. Drag tiles to the board and spell out a word.
    • Your word must match one in the game's dictionary.
    • Your titles must be placed in contiguous spaces on the board.
  2. Your score will be the sum of the values on the titles you play. If a title is placed on a DOUBLE WORD space, the base value of a valid word will be doubled and added to your score.
